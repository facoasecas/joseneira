About

In 1984 José Neira received his BFA from SUNY College at Purchase, NY, where he was awarded The Dean’s Commendation for Excellence in Design. Following, he received his MFA from the School of Art at Yale, where he had the privilege of learning directly from Paul Rand, Shigeo Fukuda, Armin Hofmann and other giants in the landscape of graphic design.

José returned to Santiago, Chile, in 1991. Since then, he has been working in the arts and culture sector, as well as in the private sector, through brand, packaging, editorial, and signage design. Parallel to that, he has been involved in higher education, as an adjunct faculty member at Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile, Universidad de Chile, Universidad Finis Terrae, and Universidad Andrés Bello. He was appointed Director of the latter’s Design School in 2007, a position he held until 2009.
